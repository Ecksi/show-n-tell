Founded in 2015 by 35th generation Shaolin Master Shi Heng Chao, the Denver Shaolin Kung Fu Academy offers authentic, traditional Shaolin martial arts training in Denver, Colorado.

The academy offers classes in Shaolin Kung Fu, Tai Chi, Qi Gong, Qin Na and San Da.

The academy is open to students of all ages, backgrounds and athletic abilities. Students learn directly from Shifu Shi Heng Chao and all students study the full Shaolin curriculum. This includes internal (Qi Gong and Tai Chi) and external training (Kung Fu, Qin Na and San Da). Shi Heng Chao believes deeply in the benefits of the entire spectrum of Shaolin training.
